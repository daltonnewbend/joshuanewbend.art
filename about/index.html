Joshua Newbend: An Artist at Play

Growing up in the South can present many unique challenges. Throughout my life, I have become emboldened by my experiences and inspired by my surroundings. I have been making art since I was young and asking questions well before that. My curious nature and sense of wonder at heart help to influence where I get inspiration and how I make work. Since receiving my BFA from Columbus State University, I have been working on expanding my current portfolio and showing my art in exhibitions. My work mainly focuses on ideas of play, childhood versus adulthood, and natural themes, such as galls. I love to create in three dimensions as it is a way for me to connect with work and see it from all angles. Let me show you what I can do!
